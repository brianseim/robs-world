## Feats & Special Abilities

**Feats:**
- **Scribe Scroll** – Create scrolls that you or another spellcaster can use (PH pg 99)
- **Spell Mastery** – Know certain spells so well you don't need a spellbook to prepare them (PH pg 100). Spells affected marked with \* in [[Spellbook]]
- **Parry** – Skilled at deflecting attacks (Rob's Snowfeats pg 4)
- **Toughness** – +3 HP (PH pg 101)
- **Simple Weapon Proficiency (Quarterstaff)** – Class/Feat (PH pg 100)
- **Alertness** *(from familiar [[Labraen]])* – +2 on Spot and Listen checks while Labraen is near (PH pg 83)
- **Spell Focus (Illusion)** – +1 to save DCs for illusion spells (PH pgs 53 & 99)

**Racial Abilities (Moon Elf):**
- Immune to magical sleep effects (PH pg 16)
- +2 save vs. enchantment spells/effects (PH pg 15)
- Low-light vision (PH pg 16)
- +2 to Listen, Search, and Spot checks (PH pg 16)

**Class Abilities:**
- Summon Familiar (PH pg 57) → [[Labraen]]
