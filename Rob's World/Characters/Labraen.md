## Special Qualities & Skills

**Special Qualities:** Low-light vision

**Skills:**
- Listen: +16 (+8 racial bonus on Listen checks)
- Move Silently: +17 (+14 racial bonus)
- Spot: +8 (+4 racial bonus on Spot; +3 additional in shadowy illumination — this +3 bonus is also granted to master [[Pfineas Starmantle]])

**Feats:** Alertness, Weapon Finesse^B

> [!note] Weapon Finesse — Bonus Feat
> Per Monster Manual v.3.5 Errata (pub. 2/17/2006), Weapon Finesse is a Bonus Feat for the Owl (indicated by superscript B). Bonus feats do not require prerequisites (see MM pg 301). An additional feat is added per the official errata — consult the DM for details.

## Combat Notes

Owls swoop quietly down onto prey, attacking with their powerful talons.

**Racial Skill Bonuses:**
- +8 racial bonus on Listen checks
- +4 racial bonus on Spot checks
- +14 racial bonus on Move Silently checks
- \*Additional +3 racial bonus on Spot checks in areas of shadowy illumination (this +3 bonus also applies to the master of an Owl familiar)

---

## Familiar Benefits to Pfineas

- **Alertness Feat** – While Labraen is within arm's reach, [[Pfineas Starmantle]] gains +2 on Spot and Listen checks
- **+3 Spot in shadows** – Granted by Labraen's racial trait
- Identify spell uses Owl Feather as a material component (100 gp pearl + owl feather)
